Methodology

Each figure is anchored to a sourced value and a rate of change, then extrapolated in your browser. The BTC price is live from CoinGecko; everything else updates when the underlying data is refreshed. This is an estimate engine, not a settlement system — see js/data.js for every number, its date, and its source.
